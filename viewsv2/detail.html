Chronicles of the Azure Realm

By Akira Tanaka  

Status Ongoing

Synopsis

In a world where magic flows through crystalline formations called Azure Stones, young Kai discovers he possesses the rare ability to communicate with these ancient artifacts. As dark forces threaten to shatter the balance between realms, Kai must master his powers and unite unlikely allies to prevent the collapse of everything he holds dear. A tale of friendship, courage, and the unbreakable bonds that connect all living things.
