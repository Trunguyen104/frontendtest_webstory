Kai stood before the shimmering crystal, his hand trembling as he reached out. The energy pulsed against his fingertips, ancient and alive. It had chosen him.

"You are the Azure Bearer," the voice whispered from within the stone, echoing through the chamber. The fate of the realms now rested in his hands.

Behind him, footsteps approached. Allies or enemies—Kai did not know. But one thing was certain: he could not turn back now.
